Login & Registration ​

ClawdSaber uses email magic links for authentication. There are no passwords to remember — just enter your email, click the link, and you are in.

If your email is not yet registered, an account is created automatically on first login.

How to Log In ​

  1. Open ClawdSaber.
  2. Enter your email address on the login screen.
  3. Click Send Magic Link.
  4. The screen changes to a "waiting for email" state.
  5. Check your inbox and click the link.

Two Ways to Complete Login ​

Method A: Click the Link Directly ​

If your system supports deep links, clicking the link in the email opens ClawdSaber automatically and completes login.

The deep link uses the clawsaber:// protocol. If your OS prompts you to allow it, confirm to proceed.

Method B: Copy and Paste ​

If clicking the link opens a browser page instead:

  1. Copy the full URL from the email.
  2. Switch back to ClawdSaber.
  3. Paste the URL into the input field shown on the waiting screen.
  4. Click Verify.

What Happens After Login ​

  • First-time user: The setup wizard appears. See Quick Start.
  • Returning user: You go directly to the chat page.

Language Selection ​

The login page has a language selector in the top-right corner. Three languages are supported:

  • English
  • Chinese
  • Japanese

You can also change the language later in Settings.

Common Issues ​

I did not receive the email ​

  • Check your spam or junk folder.
  • Make sure you entered the correct email address.
  • Wait a moment and try again — sometimes delivery takes a minute.

The link does not open the app ​

  • Copy the full URL and paste it into the app manually (Method B).
  • Make sure ClawdSaber is still running when you click the link.

The link expired ​

Magic links are valid for a limited time. If the link has expired, go back to the login screen and request a new one.

I am stuck on the login screen after clicking the link ​

  • Try copying and pasting the link instead.
  • Restart ClawdSaber and request a fresh magic link.
